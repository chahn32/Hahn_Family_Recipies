# Good Old Fashioned Holiday Spirit

**Nutrition (per cocktail):** 150 cal | 0g fat | 6g carbs | Contains alcohol

**Ingredients:**
- 2 oz Sweet Mash Rye Whiskey
- 1 tsp simple syrup
- 2 dashes Peychaud's bitters
- 2 orange peels
- Luxardo cherry
- Large ice cube

**Instructions:**
1. Add Sweet Mash Rye Whiskey, simple syrup, bitters, and orange peel to mixing glass, top with ice, and stir
2. Strain into rocks glass over large ice cube
3. Top with orange peel and Luxardo cherry
