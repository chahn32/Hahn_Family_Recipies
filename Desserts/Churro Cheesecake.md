# Churro Cheesecake

**Nutrition (per serving, 12 servings):** 420 cal | 28g fat | 38g carbs | 7g protein

**Sugar Mix:**
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon

**Whip:**
- 8 oz heavy cream
- 5 oz white chocolate, melted

**Mix on medium 30 seconds:**
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 10 oz cream cheese
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk

**Instructions:**
1. Whip cream ingredients until peaks form
2. Mix cream cheese mixture ingredients on medium speed
3. Fold whipped cream into cream cheese mixture until combined
4. Preheat oven to 350°F
5. Use Pillsbury crescents or crescent dough sheet
6. Spray cooking spray on bottom of pan
7. Layer sugar mix to coat bottom
8. Layer dough on top of sugar
9. Pour filling on top of dough
10. Spray cooking spray on top of filling
11. Add another dough layer on top
12. Spray cooking spray on top of dough
13. Add final coat of sugar on top of dough
14. Bake 30 minutes at 350°F
15. Refrigerate overnight
