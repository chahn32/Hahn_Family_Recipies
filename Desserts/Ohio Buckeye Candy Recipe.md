# Ohio Buckeye Candy Recipe

**Nutrition (per piece, ~100 pieces):** 85 cal | 5g fat | 9g carbs | 2g protein

**Ingredients:**
- 1 lb. peanut butter
- Melt 3 sticks butter
- Add 2 lbs. confectioner's sugar

**Instructions:**
1. Mix well and make into balls
2. Melt 12 oz. package chocolate chips with 1/2 bar paraffin wax and stir over low heat
3. Dip balls into mixture. Let dry on wax paper
4. Makes approximately 100 pieces

*ENJOY!*
