# Simple Chocolate Chip Cookies

**Nutrition (per cookie, ~36 cookies):** 165 cal | 8g fat | 22g carbs | 2g protein

**Ingredients:**
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup margarine
- 1¼ cup sugar
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2¼ cup flour
- ¾ tsp soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 12 oz chocolate chips

**Directions:**
Cream together butter, margarine, and both sugars. Add eggs and mix. Add vanilla. Combine dry ingredients and mix until just combined; do not over mix. Add in chocolate chips. Chill dough for at least 30 minutes. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 8-12 minutes. Cool on baking sheet for one minute, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
