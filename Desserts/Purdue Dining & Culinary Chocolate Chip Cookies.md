# Purdue Dining & Culinary Chocolate Chip Cookies

**Nutrition (per cookie, ~36 cookies):** 165 cal | 8g fat | 22g carbs | 2g protein

**Ingredients:**
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup margarine
- 1¼ cup sugar
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2¼ cup flour
- ¾ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 12 oz chocolate chips

**Instructions:**
1. Cream together butter, margarine, and both sugars
2. Add eggs and mix. Add vanilla
3. Combine dry ingredients and mix until just combined; do not over mix
4. Add chocolate chips
5. Chill dough for at least 30 minutes
6. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto baking sheet
7. Bake at 350°F for 8-12 minutes
8. Cool on baking sheet for one minute, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely
