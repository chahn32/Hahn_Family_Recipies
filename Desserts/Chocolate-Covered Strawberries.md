# Chocolate-Covered Strawberries

*Easy and elegant dessert or treat*

**Ingredients:**
- Fresh strawberries
- Chocolate (milk, dark, or white)

**Instructions:**
1. Wash strawberries and pat completely dry
2. Place strawberries in ice cube tray compartments
3. Melt chocolate in microwave or double boiler
4. Pour melted chocolate over strawberries in ice cube tray
5. Refrigerate until chocolate is set
6. Pop out of ice cube tray and serve

**Tips:**
- Make sure strawberries are completely dry before dipping
- Use high-quality chocolate for best results
- Can drizzle with contrasting chocolate for decoration
