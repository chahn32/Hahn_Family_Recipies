# Fantasy Fudge

**Nutrition (per piece, ~64 pieces):** 95 cal | 4g fat | 14g carbs | 1g protein

**Ingredients:**
- 3 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2/3 cup (5 1/3 oz can) evaporated milk
- 1 (12 oz) pkg semi-sweet chocolate pieces
- 1 (7 oz) jar marshmallow creme
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 1 tsp vanilla

**Instructions:**
1. Combine sugar, butter and milk in heavy 2 1/2 or 3 quart saucepan; bring to full rolling boil, stirring constantly. Continue boiling 5 minutes over medium heat, stirring constantly to prevent scorching. Remove from heat; stir in chocolate pieces until melted. Add marshmallow creme, nuts and vanilla; beat until well blended
2. Pour into greased 13 x 9 inch pan. Cool at room temperature; cut into squares. Makes approximately 3 pounds
