# Lucille Sugar Cream Pie

*Classic Indiana sugar cream pie (Hoosier Pie) from Lucille*

## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup cornstarch
- 2 cups milk
- ½ stick margarine
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 baked 9-inch pie shell
- Nutmeg for sprinkling

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar, salt, and cornstarch
2. Add milk and margarine
3. Cook until thick, stirring constantly
4. Remove from heat and add vanilla
5. Pour into baked pie shell
6. Sprinkle with nutmeg
7. Chill until set (about 4 hours)

## Notes

- Classic Indiana sugar cream pie, also known as Hoosier pie
- Simple custard-based pie
- Indiana's unofficial state pie
