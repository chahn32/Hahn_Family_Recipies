# Thompson Basic Sugar Cookies

*Classic cut-out sugar cookies from the Thompson family - makes 7 dozen cookies*

## Ingredients

### Cookies
- 2½ cups flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1½ teaspoons grated lemon peel
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- ½ teaspoon baking powder

### Icing
- 2 cups confectioners' sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 tablespoon water
- ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar

### Food coloring (optional)
- 40 cal per teaspoon

## Instructions

1. Combine flour and salt
2. Cream butter and sugar together
3. Beat in egg, lemon peel, and vanilla
4. Add dry ingredients and mix well
5. Cover dough and refrigerate until firm
6. Roll out on floured surface
7. Cut into desired shapes
8. Bake at 350°F for 8-9 minutes
9. Makes 7 dozen cookies (45 cal each)

### For Icing
1. Mix confectioners' sugar, egg white, water, and cream of tartar
2. Beat until smooth
3. Add food coloring if desired
4. Decorate cooled cookies
