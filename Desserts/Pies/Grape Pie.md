# Grape Pie

*Prep: 35 min | Total: 3 hr 10 min | Serves 8*
**Crust:**
- 1 box (14.1 oz) refrigerated pie crusts (2 count)
- ½ teaspoon all-purpose flour

**Filling:**
- 4 cups red seedless grapes
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour

**Topping:**
- 1½ cups old-fashioned oats
- ¾ cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup butter, softened
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 egg, beaten

**Instructions:**
1. Heat oven to 425°F. Place one crust in 9-inch deep-dish pie plate
2. In 4-quart saucepan, cook all filling ingredients except ¼ cup flour over medium heat, stirring frequently, for 10 minutes until grapes are tender. Stir in flour. Set aside
3. Mix all topping ingredients except egg until crumbly. Spoon ⅔ of topping into crust. Spoon filling over topping
4. Cut second crust into ½-inch strips. Create lattice top, weaving strips. Trim and seal edges. Brush with egg. Sprinkle remaining topping over pie
5. Cover with foil and bake 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15-20 minutes longer until golden brown
6. Cool at least 2 hours before serving
