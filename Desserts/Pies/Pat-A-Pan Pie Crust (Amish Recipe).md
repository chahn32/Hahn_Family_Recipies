# Pat-A-Pan Pie Crust (Amish Recipe)

*The easiest homemade pie crust - no rolling required!*

**Ingredients:**
- 1½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1½ teaspoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 3 tablespoons cold milk

**Instructions:**
1. Place flour, sugar, and salt in a 9-inch pie pan and mix with fingertips until evenly blended
2. In a measuring cup, combine oil and milk and beat until creamy
3. Pour all at once over flour mixture. Mix with a fork until completely moistened
4. Pat the dough with your fingers, first at the sides of the plate and then across the bottom
5. Flute the edges
6. Shell is now ready to be filled

**For Pre-Baked Crust:**
7. Preheat oven to 425°F
8. Prick the surface with a fork and bake 15 minutes
9. Check often and prick more if needed
