# Teriyaki Beef Bowl

*Asian Takeout at Home*

## Ingredients

- 1 lb sirloin steak, sliced
- ¼ cup teriyaki sauce
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 cup jasmine rice
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced

## Toppings

- Green onions
- Sesame seeds
- Sriracha drizzle
- Pickled ginger

## Instructions

1. Cook jasmine rice according to package directions
2. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat
3. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant
4. Add sliced sirloin steak to the pan
5. Cook until browned on all sides, about 3-4 minutes
6. Add broccoli florets and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes
7. Pour teriyaki sauce over beef and broccoli
8. Toss to coat evenly and cook until sauce thickens slightly, about 2 minutes
9. Serve over jasmine rice
10. Garnish with green onions, sesame seeds, sriracha, and pickled ginger

## Notes

- **Hump day easy**: Better than takeout. 38g protein + meal prep friendly!
- For homemade teriyaki sauce: Mix ¼ cup soy sauce, 2 tbsp honey, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp ginger, 1 tsp cornstarch
- Can substitute chicken or tofu for beef
- Add other vegetables like snap peas, bell peppers, or carrots
- Meal prep: Store components separately and assemble when ready to eat
