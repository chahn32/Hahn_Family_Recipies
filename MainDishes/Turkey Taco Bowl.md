# Turkey Taco Bowl

*Healthy Mexican Night*

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground turkey
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 2 cups brown rice
- 1 can black beans
- 1 cup corn
- Salsa

## Toppings

- Diced avocado
- Greek yogurt (sour cream substitute)
- Shredded lettuce
- Pico de gallo
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Cook brown rice according to package directions
2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat
3. Add ground turkey and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned, about 7-8 minutes
4. Drain any excess fat
5. Add taco seasoning and follow packet directions (usually add water and simmer)
6. In separate pan or microwave, heat black beans and corn
7. To assemble bowls:
   - Start with a base of brown rice
   - Add seasoned turkey
   - Add black beans and corn
   - Top with salsa
8. Add toppings: diced avocado, Greek yogurt, shredded lettuce, pico de gallo
9. Serve with lime wedges

## Notes

- **Saturday comfort**: Lean protein fiesta. 35g protein + fiber-loaded!
- Ground turkey is leaner than ground beef but just as satisfying
- Meal prep friendly - store components separately for up to 4 days
- Can substitute ground chicken or lean ground beef
- Add jalapeños for extra heat
- Make it a taco salad by serving over lettuce instead of rice
