# Stuffed Peppers

**Nutrition (per serving, 4 servings):** 385 cal | 26g fat | 12g carbs | 24g protein

**Ingredients:**
- Bell jalapeños or poblanos
- Shredded meat, mild Italian sausage
- Diced onion & garlic
- Cream cheese
- Mozzarella or parmesan cheese, shredded

**Instructions:**
1. Cut jalapeños or peppers in half lengthwise and scoop out inside seeds and membranes
2. In a pan, brown the Italian sausage with diced onion and garlic
3. Mix cream cheese into the sausage mixture (about 5 minutes before sausage is fully cooked)
4. Stuff the pepper halves with the sausage and cheese mixture
5. Place stuffed peppers in baking dish
6. Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes
7. Peppers should be soft when tested with a fork
8. Top with shredded mozzarella or parmesan cheese
9. Turn oven to broil and bake until cheese is melted and golden, watching carefully
