# One-Pan Chicken Fajitas

*15-Minute Family Favorite*

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
- 3 bell peppers, sliced (use different colors for visual appeal)
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 tbsp fajita seasoning
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Lime juice

## Toppings

- Sour cream
- Guacamole
- Shredded cheese
- Cilantro
- Warm tortillas

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat
2. Add sliced chicken breast to the pan
3. Season with fajita seasoning and cook until chicken is browned and nearly cooked through, about 5-7 minutes
4. Add sliced bell peppers and onion to the pan
5. Continue cooking, stirring frequently, until vegetables are tender-crisp and chicken is fully cooked, about 5-8 minutes
6. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the mixture
7. Serve immediately with warm tortillas and desired toppings

## Notes

- **Tuesday tradition**: High protein, low effort. 32g protein + colorful veggies!
- Use pre-sliced vegetables to save even more time
- Can substitute shrimp or steak for chicken
- Make it spicy with jalapeños or hot sauce
- Leftovers make great meal prep bowls over rice
