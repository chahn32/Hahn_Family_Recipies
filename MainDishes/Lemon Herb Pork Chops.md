# Lemon Herb Pork Chops

*Juicy & Flavorful*

## Ingredients

- 4 bone-in pork chops
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- Fresh rosemary
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Green beans

## Toppings

- Fresh parsley
- Lemon zest
- Garlic butter
- Crispy shallots

## Instructions

1. Pat pork chops dry with paper towels
2. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
4. Add pork chops and sear for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown
5. Reduce heat to medium
6. Add minced garlic, fresh rosemary, and lemon juice to the pan
7. Spoon the lemon-herb mixture over the pork chops as they cook
8. Continue cooking until pork chops reach 145°F internal temperature, about 5-7 more minutes
9. Meanwhile, cook green beans in the same pan around the pork chops
10. Remove from heat and let rest 3-5 minutes
11. Garnish with fresh parsley, lemon zest, garlic butter, and crispy shallots

## Notes

- **Thursday simplicity**: Restaurant quality at home. 30g protein + tender perfection!
- Don't overcook - pork chops should be slightly pink in the center
- Use a meat thermometer for perfect doneness (145°F)
- Let rest before cutting to retain juices
- For extra flavor, marinate pork chops for 30 minutes before cooking
