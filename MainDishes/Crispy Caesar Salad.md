# Crispy Caesar Salad

*Classic Caesar with crispy chicken*

## Dressing

- ½ cup mayo
- ¼ cup grated parmesan
- 3 cloves minced garlic
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper
- 1 tbsp dijon mustard
- 2-4 tbsp olive oil

## Salad

- 2 cups romaine lettuce
- Handful of cherry tomatoes
- Handful of croutons
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Caesar dressing
- Crispy chicken
- Parmesan cheese to top

## Instructions

### Make the Dressing:
1. Add all ingredients except olive oil to a bowl and whisk until smooth
2. Add olive oil 1 tbsp at a time while whisking
3. Continue until dressing reaches desired consistency

### Assemble the Salad:
1. Wash and chop romaine lettuce
2. Add lettuce to large bowl
3. Add cherry tomatoes and croutons
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add Caesar dressing and toss to coat
6. Top with crispy chicken (grilled, baked, or air-fried)
7. Garnish with extra parmesan cheese

## Notes

- For crispy chicken: Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Pan-fry or air-fry until golden and cooked through
- Make dressing ahead and store in refrigerator up to 1 week
- Add anchovies to dressing for traditional flavor
- Toast bread cubes with olive oil and garlic for homemade croutons
