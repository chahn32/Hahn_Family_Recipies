# Vietnamese-Style Baked Chicken

*Prep: 5 min | Cook: 45 min | Marinate: 30 min | Total: 80 min | Serves: 4*

**Ingredients:**
- 2 pounds bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ¼ cup Asian fish sauce
- ¼ cup palm or dark brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons grapeseed oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, finely chopped
- 1½ tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1 teaspoon lime zest
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
- 2 teaspoons hot chile paste (sambal oelek)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- Sliced limes and cilantro for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Place thighs in large zipper-lock bag
2. Whisk together soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, oil, cilantro, lime juice and zest, ginger, chile paste, and garlic
3. Pour marinade into bag, seal, and toss to coat. Marinate 30 minutes to 4 hours
4. Preheat oven to 425°F with rack in middle position
5. Line rimmed baking sheet with foil and place wire rack on top
6. Remove chicken from bag, letting marinade drip off. Set on rack with space between thighs. Discard marinade
7. Bake until thighs register 155-160°F in center, about 35 minutes
8. If skin needs more browning, broil on high for 1 minute
9. Transfer to platter and rest 5 minutes
10. Garnish with lime slices and cilantro
