# Honey Mustard Chicken

*Sweet & Tangy Winner*

## Ingredients

- 4 chicken breasts
- ¼ cup honey
- 3 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Baby potatoes

## Toppings

- Fresh thyme
- Cracked pepper
- Honey drizzle
- Roasted carrots

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F
2. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, Dijon mustard, olive oil, and minced garlic
3. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper
4. Place chicken in a baking dish
5. Brush honey mustard sauce generously over chicken breasts
6. Arrange baby potatoes around the chicken
7. Toss potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper
8. Add carrots if using
9. Bake for 25-30 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F internal temperature and potatoes are tender
10. For extra glaze, brush chicken with more sauce halfway through cooking
11. Garnish with fresh thyme, cracked pepper, and a honey drizzle

## Notes

- **Sunday prep**: Sweet meets savory. 32g protein + comfort food vibes!
- Can use chicken thighs for juicier results
- Add Brussels sprouts or green beans for more vegetables
- Leftovers are great for meal prep bowls or sandwiches
- For crispier chicken, sear in a pan first before baking
