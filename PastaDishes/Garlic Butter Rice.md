# Garlic Butter Rice

*Prep: 5 min | Cook: 25 min | Serves: 4*

**Ingredients:**
- 4 tablespoons (2 oz) butter, divided
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1½ cups uncooked white rice (long grain, Basmati, or Jasmine)
- 2½ cups chicken broth or vegetable stock
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
- 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley, plus extra to garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in medium pot over medium heat
2. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute
3. Stir in rice to coat with garlic butter
4. Add broth, dried parsley, salt, and pepper. Stir well and bring to boil
5. Cover with lid and reduce heat to low. Simmer until liquid absorbed, about 15 minutes
6. Uncover and stir through fresh parsley
7. Remove from heat, cover, and let stand 5-10 minutes until soft
8. Fluff with fork. Stir through remaining butter
9. Season with extra salt, pepper, and dried parsley
10. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley
