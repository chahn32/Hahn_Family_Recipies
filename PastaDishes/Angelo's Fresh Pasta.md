# Angelo's Fresh Pasta

*Yields approximately 12-16 oz*

**Ingredients:**
- 9 oz Janie's Mill Semolina Flour
- 4.5 oz water

**Instructions:**
1. Place flour in mixing bowl and move to outer edges to form a well
2. Pour half the water in center. Slowly mix flour and water with hands
3. Pour rest of water and finish mixing
4. After 2 minutes, pack dough into a ball
5. Dust table with flour and knead dough, pressing with heel of hand and pushing away
6. Fold, flip, and rotate throughout. Knead for about 10 minutes
7. Cover with cling wrap and rest for at least 15 minutes
8. Dust table again and roll out dough with rolling pin into a thin sheet
9. Once at desired thickness, use pizza cutter to cut pasta to desired shape
10. Lightly dust to prevent sticking
11. Store in refrigerator until ready to cook

**Storage:**
- If cooking within 24 hours: refrigerate
- If unable to cook within 24 hours: freeze and cook directly from frozen (do not thaw)
