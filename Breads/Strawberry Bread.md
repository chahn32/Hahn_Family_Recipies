# Strawberry Bread

*Prep: 30 min | Cook: 45 min | Ready in: 1 hr 15 min*
**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups fresh strawberries
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1¼ cups vegetable oil
- 4 eggs, beaten
- 1¼ cups chopped pecans

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter and flour two 9x5-inch loaf pans
2. Slice strawberries and place in medium bowl. Sprinkle lightly with sugar and set aside
3. Combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, salt, and baking soda in large bowl; mix well
4. Blend oil and eggs into flour mixture. Add strawberry mixture and pecans
5. Divide batter into pans
6. Bake until tester comes out clean, 45-50 minutes
7. Cool in pans on wire rack for 10 minutes, then turn out and cool before slicing
