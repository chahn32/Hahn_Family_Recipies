# The Best Banana Bread

*Level: Easy | Total: 1 hr 30 min | Yield: 1 loaf*
**Ingredients:**
- ½ cup melted unsalted butter (plus more for greasing)
- 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup toasted pecans, chopped
- ½ cup granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon fine salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
- 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
- ¼ cup buttermilk, sour cream, or yogurt
- ½ cup light brown sugar, lightly packed
- 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
- 4 soft, very ripe bananas, mashed (about 1½ cups)

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter one 9x5-inch loaf pan
2. Whisk together flour, pecans, granulated sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, salt, and nutmeg in a large bowl
3. Whisk together eggs, melted butter, buttermilk, brown sugar, and vanilla in a medium bowl; stir in mashed bananas
4. Fold banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined (lumps are okay)
5. Pour batter into buttered pan and lightly tap on counter
6. Bake until browned and a toothpick comes out clean, about 1 hour
7. Cool 10 minutes in pan, then turn out onto rack to cool completely
