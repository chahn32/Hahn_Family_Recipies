# Strawberry Bread (Alternate Version)

**Ingredients:**
- 2 (10-ounce) packages frozen strawberries, thawed and undrained
- 4 eggs
- 1¼ cups oil
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 teaspoons cinnamon
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup pecans

**Instructions:**
1. Mix together strawberries, eggs, and oil
2. Sift dry ingredients into mixture. Stir to blend thoroughly
3. Stir in pecans
4. Pour into 2 greased 9x5-inch loaf pans or 3 medium or 4 small loaf pans
5. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour or until pick comes out clean
6. To keep nuts from falling to bottom, reserve a little flour and mix with nuts

*Laverne Casey, North Vernon*
