# Protein Power Overnight Oats

*Prep tonight, grab-and-go tomorrow*

## Ingredients

- ½ cup rolled oats
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp almond butter

## Toppings

- Fresh berries
- Sliced banana
- Cinnamon
- Honey drizzle

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a jar or container
2. Mix well until protein powder is fully incorporated
3. Seal and refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours)
4. In the morning, stir and add desired toppings
5. Enjoy cold or heat briefly if preferred

## Notes

- **Pro tip**: Make 5 jars on Sunday for the entire work week of breakfasts
- Can substitute any milk (dairy, oat, soy, etc.)
- Add more liquid if you prefer thinner consistency
- Store in refrigerator up to 5 days
