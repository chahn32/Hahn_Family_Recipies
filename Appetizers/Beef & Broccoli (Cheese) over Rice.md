# Beef & Broccoli (Cheese) over Rice

**Nutrition (per serving, 4 servings):** 420 cal | 12g fat | 48g carbs | 32g protein

**Ingredients:**
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 lb beef stir fry, cut into strips
- 1 can (10Â¾ oz) Campbell's Condensed Tomato Soup
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 package (10 oz) frozen (slummed) broccoli
- 1 package of broccoli seems while rice (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef and stir until well browned
2. Stir the soup, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic powder & in bowl and mix together. Pour mixture onto beef in skillet and heat to boil
3. Cook broccoli in microwave according to package directions. Stir in broccoli in with beef & soup mixture
4. Cook bags of rice according to package directions
5. Serve beef & broccoli over white rice
