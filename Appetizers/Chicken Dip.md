# Chicken Dip

**Nutrition (per serving, ~12 servings):** 180 cal | 14g fat | 6g carbs | 9g protein

**Ingredients:**
- 1 canned chicken (white meat only)
- Pizza sauce (as needed)
- 3 packs cheese cream
- 2 cans green chili (mild)

**Instructions:**
1. Put all ingredients in crockpot at slow or medium heat
2. Cook for about 30 minutes or until hot
3. Microwave a couple minutes also works
4. Serve with 1 bag of tortilla chips
