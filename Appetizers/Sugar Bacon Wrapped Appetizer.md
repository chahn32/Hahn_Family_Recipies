# Sugar Bacon Wrapped Appetizer

**Nutrition (per piece, ~24 pieces):** 120 cal | 8g fat | 6g carbs | 6g protein

**Ingredients:**
- 1 package bacon, wrapped appetizer
- 1 package beef lil smokies
- 1 package Pepperoni Bacon
- 1 package (small) dark brown sugar
- Toothpicks

**Instructions:**
1. Cut bacon in half
2. Wrap lil smokey with bacon
3. Put toothpick through bacon & lil smokey
4. Roll bacon wrapped lil smokey in brown sugar
5. Bake at 350Â°F until bacon is crispy
