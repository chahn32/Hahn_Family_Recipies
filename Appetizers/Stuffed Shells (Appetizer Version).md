# Stuffed Shells (Appetizer Version)

**Ingredients:**
- 1 jar (32 oz) Alfredo Sauce
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 2 cups cooked broccoli, chopped
- 1 cup shredded (mild) cheddar cheese
- ½ cup shredded parmesan
- 21 jumbo shells, cooked according to package
- 1½-2 cups spaghetti sauce

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Use a large bowl to mix Alfredo, chicken, broccoli, and cheese
3. Spoon the mixture you made into pasta shells
4. Grease 13x9 baking dish with butter or cooking spray
5. Spread spaghetti sauce on bottom of baking dish
6. Arrange shells in baking pan
7. Cover with foil and bake 35-40 minutes

**Shells Continued:**
- Fork pepper should be soft, then put shredded mozzarella or parmesan cheese
- While thing is like a van (Ramen) bake to excel
