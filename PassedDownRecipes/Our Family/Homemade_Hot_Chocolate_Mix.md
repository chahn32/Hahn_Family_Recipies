# Homemade Hot Chocolate Mix

*A homemade hot chocolate mix perfect for gifting or keeping on hand for cozy winter drinks*

## Ingredients

- 1 (20.1-ounce) container Nesquik chocolate beverage mix (about 5.5 cups)
- 1 (16-ounce) container powdered non-dairy coffee creamer (about 3.5 cups)
- 1 (32-ounce) container instant nonfat dry milk (about 7.5 cups)
- 1 (16-ounce) bag powdered sugar (about 4 cups)
- 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- Freeze dried marshmallows (optional)

## Instructions

1. In a very large bowl, combine all ingredients well
2. Divide into individual sealable, food-safe containers
3. Top with a scoop of freeze-dried marshmallows, if desired

### To Serve
- Combine ½ cup mix with ½ cup boiling water. Stir well
- **Note:** Can also use ½ cup hot milk for richer taste

## Notes

- Makes 42 servings (½ cup mix per serving)
- Perfect for holiday gift giving
- Store in airtight containers
- Add marshmallows to containers for a festive touch
