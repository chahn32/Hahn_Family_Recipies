# Thompson Coffee Cake

*A classic family coffee cake recipe from the Thompson family*

## Ingredients

### Cake
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2  cup sugar
- 1/2 cup oil
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk

### Topping
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons butter (additional)

## Instructions

1. Mix together dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
2. Pour into greased 9-inch square pan
3. Reserve ¼ cup of batter
4. Add remaining batter, add topping ingredients, and drop onto batter
5. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes

## Notes

- The original recipe card shows "350° 30 min" as baking instructions
- This appears to be a simple dump-and-stir coffee cake with a crumb topping
