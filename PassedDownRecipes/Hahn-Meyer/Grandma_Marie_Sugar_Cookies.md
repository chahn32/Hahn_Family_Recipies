# Grandma Marie Sugar Cookies

*Classic sugar cookies from Grandma Marie*

## Ingredients

- 1½ cups powdered sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- ½ teaspoon almond extract
- 2½ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar

## Instructions

1. Cream Butter and sugar, add egg and (Something, I cant make out)
2. disolve baking soda in milk, add to mixture
3. Add vanilla
3. Stir in Flour, slowly add salt and lemon extract. Chill ½ hour
4. Roll to ¼" thick. Dip cutters in flour before lovely cuttings
5. Bake 8 min. at 350° Makes 4 doz.
