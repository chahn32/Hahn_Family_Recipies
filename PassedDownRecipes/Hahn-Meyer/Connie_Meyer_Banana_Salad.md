# Connie Meyer Banana Salad

*A sweet and creamy banana salad from Connie Meyer*

## Ingredients

- 4 cups milk
- 4 egg yolks
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 tablespoon (heaping) corn starch
- Pinch of salt
- 4-6 bananas

## Instructions

1. Mix together salt, flour, sugar, cornstarch in a saucepan
2. Add small amount of milk to egg yolks and mix well. Should be thick
3. Add remaining milk
4. Heat, stirring until thick. Add vanilla after it's cooled
5. Cool, add bananas and top with whipped cream

## Notes

- The original recipe card shows handwritten instructions
- Recipe appears to be a custard-based banana pudding/salad
