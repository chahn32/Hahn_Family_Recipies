# Grape Pie

*Traditional Concord grape pie*

## Ingredients

- 5⅓ - 5½ cups Concord grapes
- 1½ cups sugar
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Pie crust for double crust pie

## Instructions

1. Remove skins from grapes and save pulp from grapes
2. Put pulp into saucepan without water and bring to rolling boil
3. While hot, run through strainer to remove seeds
4. Mix strained pulp well with skins
5. Mix together sugar, flour, lemon juice, and butter
6. Cook through with grapes
7. Pour into pie crust
8. Bake at 250°F for 35-45 minutes

## Notes

- Traditional Concord grape pie
- Requires removing skins and seeds from grapes
