# Easy Burgers

*Quick and easy burgers from Mrs. Todd*

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs, slightly beaten
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion (1 teaspoon, minced dry)
- 1 pound ground beef
- ¼ cup milk

## Instructions

1. Blend ingredients - drop from spoon on a hot greased skillet. Spread out to flatten
2. Brown over medium heat, turn and brown on other side. Cooking takes about 5 minutes

## Notes

- Recipe is from Mrs. Todd
